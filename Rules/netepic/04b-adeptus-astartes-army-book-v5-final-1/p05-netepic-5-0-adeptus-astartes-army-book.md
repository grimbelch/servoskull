# NetEpic 5.0 Adeptus Astartes Army Book

### **Special Rules**

###### **<mark>Optional Rule: Traditional Companies</mark>**

<mark>Traditionally, a Chapter is composed of ten infantry companies of specific types. The best and most experienced soldiers are collected in a Chapter's 1</mark><sup>st</sup> <mark>Company, and take the field as either Veteran Marines or Terminators. These are the best of the best, and only one Company of Veterans or Terminators may be fielded. Similarly, the 10</mark><sup>th</sup> <mark>Company contains the young and inexperienced and is composed of Scout Marines.</mark>

<mark>A standard Chapter has one Veteran or Terminator, four Battle, two Tactical, one Assault, one Devastator and one Scout Companies. You can choose to field any and as many of these that you want, choosing and paying for the Company Cards as normal, but you cannot exceed the number of each type of company available. This restriction does not apply to Support Cards: any company may take tanks, artillery, fliers and so on. Only the Companies are limited in number, i.e. an army can never have more than one Veteran Company, though Veteran Detachments may be bought as support for any company.</mark>

###### **<mark>Optional Rule: Footsloggers</mark>**

<mark>Players may purchase non-mechanized infantry companies. The existing company cards include Rhinos, and a player may drop 9 Rhinos (the commander keeps his) and reduce the cost of the company by 100 points. If this option is taken, the troops may not start the game loaded on any other type of transport (such as Thunderhawks or Drop Pods).</mark>

###### **<mark>Optional Rule: Pre-Heresy Armies</mark>**

<mark>Many weapons of war have been developed since the Horus Heresy. If you'd like to create a historically accurate army list, you may only purchase the following units:</mark>

**<mark>Infantry:</mark>** <mark>Assault, Devastator & Tactical Marine, Terminator</mark> **<mark>Cavalry:</mark>** <mark>Bike, Land Speeder</mark> **<mark>Walkers:</mark>** <mark>Mutilator & Destroyer Dreadnaught Squad, Robot</mark> **<mark>Vehicles:</mark>** <mark>Drop Pods, Land Raider, Predator, Rhino, Vindicator</mark> **<mark>Light Artillery:</mark>** <mark>Rapier, Mole Mortar, Tarantula, Thudd Gun</mark> **<mark>Heavy Artillery:</mark>** <mark>Whirlwind</mark> **<mark>Titans:</mark>** <mark>All</mark>
