# NetEpic 5.0 Core Rules

#### **Background**

NetEpic is a 6 mm tabletop wargame of science fiction combat, set in the Games Workshop Warhammer 40,000 universe. Two or more players each command an army and battle for supremacy. The rules are divided into two parts: the basic game mechanics (this book), and numerous army books that contain background, special rules and troop descriptions. Throughout these books you'll find many optional rules. In order to use them, all players must agree to them before the game.

###### **Greetings NetEpic Gamers Everywhere!**

Every so often a small idea is born and grows to such a degree that it takes a life of its own NetEpic is such an idea.

Over seven years ago on the old Majordomo Space Marine mailing list the concept of updating the second edition epic rules with input of epic enthusiasts from all over the world was first coined. It was given even more impetus due to the release of new edition of epic (Epic 40k), which was not favored by the majority of epic gamers. Instead of the grumbling and sterile arguments common at that time over what version was better, the original core of "founding fathers" formed their own mailing list to update, overhaul and generally "tweak" the version of the rules we loved. Thus the "NetEpic Project was born.

Our initial goals were humble in extreme. We vowed that if even one gamer tried, played and liked what we have done, it would be an effort well served. Happily the goal was quickly and easily met. In 10 months of its beginnings the first version of the NetEpic rules was available. Its exposure was magnified by the addition of NetEpic's very own website - The Epicentre (www.netepic.org). Now our rules had a truly global reach, available to any who wanted to download it.

Since those early days we have come far and done much. In your hands you hold our latest (and we hope greatest) version - 5.0. This version includes all the new models that have been released in recent times as well as a whole host of new unit and army lists that reflect the changes of the 40k universe since the second edition first saw the light of day.

Of particular note is the brand new Tau army list as well as the addition of the whole line of new Forgeworld model status for their immediate use. Add to this newly done army cards and counters and NetEpic 5.0 is a grand project made to look as great as it plays!

NetEpic, by its nature, is a team effort. Each book represents the time and input of many epic gamers just like you. As with any effort, some individuals, by the weight of their contributions, deserve a special mention. Jarreas Underwood, our esteemed editor, who has lavished untold hours to produce the remarkable work you now have before you. Without a web presence, there would be no "net" in NetEpic if not for the tireless efforts of Tom Webb, our webmaster. My heartfelt thanks, and the thanks from the hundreds, (and maybe thousands), of people who depend and enjoy your efforts.

A special thanks to all our esteemed NetEpic mailing list members. It takes a special type of person to want to contribute to the project. Our motto "put your money where your mouth is" underlines that our list is about DOING, not just talking. You can all take pride in this work and the knowledge on how many people will have fun because of your contributions.

Finally, thanks to all of YOU! NetEpic success is greatly due to all the fans who throughout the years have supported us with words of praise, constructive criticism and helpful ideas. You are the reason why we continue this work!

Long Live NetEpic!

Peter Ramos NetEpic Coordinator
