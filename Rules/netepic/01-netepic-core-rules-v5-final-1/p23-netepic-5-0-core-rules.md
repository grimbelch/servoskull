# NetEpic 5.0 Core Rules

###### **<mark>Special Rule: Destroying Barricades, Trenches & Minefields</mark>**

<mark>Barricades and Trenches, unlike other buildings, cannot be destroyed by artillery. They may be destroyed by Engineers, "Damages/Destroys Buildings" weapons or by anything the scours a large section of ground. This includes Deathrollers, Gorgon blades and passing titans & praetorians. These units do not need to have any special orders to remove the obstacle - they just take it out on the way past. Minefields are cleared in the same way, but the clearing unit will suffer D6 automatic hits. Providing the unit survives, it will clear a path through the minefield as wide as the model.</mark>
