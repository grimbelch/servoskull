# NetEpic 5.0 Slaan Army Book

## **The Slaan Army**

A Slaan army represents a heterogeneous force that may be composed of True Slann, their slave race the Necrons, Exodus Slann or their slave race the Dracons. A Slaan army may be composed of any or all of these forces.

The Slaan are a Codex army, but as they have been recruiting from all the younger races they may pick more than one race as allies. You must put at least 50% of your points into Slaan units (any of the ones in this book), and may put the other 50% into any number of Standard Lists. Each ally must satisfy the army construction rules (e.g. one Special and five Support Cards per Company).

|**Codex Army**|**At least 50% of your points into...**|**Up to 50% into...**|
|---|---|---|
|Slaan|Slaan ArmyBook|Anyother Standard Listsyou want|

##### **<mark>Special Rule: Slaan Support Cards</mark>**

<mark>There are inherent differences in command and control among the different "arms" of the Slann army. Therefore there are some restrictions as to which Special & Support Cards may be associated with the various Company Cards. These are:</mark>

<mark>A) True Slaan cards may only be attached to True Slaan Company Cards.</mark>

<mark>B) Necron cards may only be attached to Necron or True Slaan Company Cards. C) Exodus cards may only be attached to Exodus or True Slaan Company Cards. D) Dracon cards may only be attached to Exodus, Dracon or True Slaan Company Cards.</mark>

**<mark>Note:</mark>** <mark>The Slann Army Book is new and should be considered optional. You must have the permission of your opponent before using it.</mark>
