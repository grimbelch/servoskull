The NetEpic project is a group effort and individual contributors are not usually mentioned. However, it would be unfair to ignore that the major creative effort regarding the background and adaptation of the Slann to NetEpic has been done by one member, Teras Cassidy. In close collaboration, Peter Ramos created many of the new Necron units, and Darius Spano and Ed Heidal have provided both significant contributions & extensive playtesting.
