# Net Epic 5.0 Tau Army Book

### **The Tau Army**

The Standard Tau Army consists of Kroot mercenaries. Kroot forces may be found and hired by many races across the galaxy, and are hired by a wide variety of forces. All units labeled as Kroot are part of the Standard List, and all others are not. If you choose to run a Kroot army you must put at least 50% of your points into the Kroot units from this book. The other 50% may be spent on any one Standard List.

**<u>Standard Army At least 50% of your points into... Up to 50% into...</u>** Kroot Mercenaries Kroot units from the Tau Army Book Any one Standard List

The Tau themselves are a Codex army and you must put at least 75% of your points into Tau units (any units from this book), and you may put up to 25% into any one Standard List.

|**Codex Army**|**At least 75% of your points into...**|**Up to 25% into...**|
|---|---|---|
|Tau|Tau ArmyBook|Anyone Standard List|

###### **<mark>Special Rule: Kroot Support</mark>**

<mark>Kroot companies may only take Kroot Support and Special Cards. Tau companies may take Kroot units as support, but should probably bring along a Shaper to keep them in hand.</mark>

###### **<mark>Special Rule: Ta'lissera Ritual</mark>**

<mark>Certain Tau infantry units may perform a bonding ritual. This increases their Break Point to 75% (instead of the normal 50%) and the increases the VP they give when broken by 50%. The points cost and VP for a bonded unit is listed in parentheses next to the normal stats.</mark>

**<mark>Note:</mark>** <mark>The Tau and Kroot are new army lists and should be considered optional. You must have the permission of</mark> <u><mark>your opponent before running them.</mark></u>
