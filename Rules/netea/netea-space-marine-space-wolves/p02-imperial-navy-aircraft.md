# **SPACE WOLVES SPACE MARINE ARMY LIST**

Space Wolf Space Marine armies have a strategy rating of 5. Blood Claws, Fenrisian Wolves, Skyclaws, Swiftclaws and Imperial Navy aircraft formations have an initiative rating of 2+ (see _Unblooded_ ). All other formations have an initiative rating of 1+.
