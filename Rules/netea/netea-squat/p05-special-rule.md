# SPECIAL RULE

**SPECIAL RULE** **_Directed_**

Directed weapons do not require a line of fire to enemy units that a _Spotter_ (see _Spotter_ ) in their unit's formation has a line of fire to. If firing without a line of fire at one of these units then the weapon has a minimum range of 30cm.

## **SPECIAL RULE**

**_Spotter_**

A Spotter allows _Directed_ weapons (see _Directed_ ) on units in their formation to fire at enemy units that it has a line of fire to.
