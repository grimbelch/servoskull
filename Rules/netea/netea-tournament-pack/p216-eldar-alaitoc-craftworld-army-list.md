### **ELDAR ALAITOC CRAFTWORLD ARMY LIST**

Eldar Alaitoc Craftworld armies have a strategy rating of 4. Avatar, Aspect Warrior, Phantom, Revenants and Warlock formations have an initiative rating of 1+. All other formations have an initiative rating of 2+.
