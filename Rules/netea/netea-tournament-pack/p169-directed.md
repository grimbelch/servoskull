# Directed

**5.36.1** **_Directed_**

Directed weapons do not require a line of fire to enemy units that a _Spotter_ (see 5.36.2) in their unit's formation has a line of fire to. If firing without a line of fire at one of these units then the weapon has a minimum range of 30cm.

**5.36.2** **_Spotter_**

A Spotter allows _Directed_ weapons (see 5.36.1) on units in their formation to fire at enemy units that it has a line of fire to.
